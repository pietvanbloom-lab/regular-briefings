WAIC 2026 opens in Shanghai with Xi Jinping's first keynote and a new China-backed AI governance body

Xi Jinping delivered the opening keynote, his first at the event, setting out four points on global AI governance and pledging 5,000 AI training places for developing countries over five years plus deployment of the Mazu meteorological early-warning system in 30 countries. The World AI Cooperation Organization was formally established at the conference, with 29 countries signing the founding agreement. This is the institutional story of the day: a parallel governance track to the Western bodies, headquartered in Shanghai. Sources: english.news.cn, techtimes.com

Huawei gives the Atlas 950 SuperPoD its world debut at WAIC

Huawei publicly unveiled the Atlas 950 SuperPoD at the conference, a large-scale training and inference cluster that links thousands of Ascend processors into a single system over high-speed interconnect. Claude Code ships background sessions for /fork and splits the in-session path into /subtask

The July 17 Claude Code release changes how branching works: /fork now copies the conversation into its own background session with a row in the agents view while you keep working, and the in-session subagent it used to launch becomes /subtask. The same release hardens safeguards around WebSearch, subagent spawns and Bash execution, and fixes background jobs failing with a not-logged-in state on LLM gateway auth after daemon respawn. This is the only first-party Anthropic product movement dated inside the window. Sources: code.claude.com, releasebot.io
