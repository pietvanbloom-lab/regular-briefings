OpenAI to acquire Ona (formerly Gitpod) to run Codex agents inside the customer's own cloud

Tier 1 High confidence Competitor / M&A

Announced June 11: OpenAI will acquire Ona, the secure cloud execution and orchestration startup formerly known as Gitpod, and fold the team into its Codex division. The technology lets Codex agents keep running in a customer-controlled cloud sandbox for hours or days after a laptop closes. OpenAI said Codex passed 5 million weekly users, up roughly 400 percent this year. Terms were not disclosed and the deal is subject to regulatory approval.
